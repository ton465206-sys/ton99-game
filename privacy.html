Privacy Policy

Jiksor Up! Wild Animals is a puzzle game for children and families.

Data collection

The game does not ask for real names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or account login.

Leaderboard

When a puzzle is completed, the game may save a score, level, animal, time, moves, and a randomly generated player tag such as "Happy Panda 42". Children should not enter real names.

Ads and purchases

This version has no third-party ads and no in-app purchases.
